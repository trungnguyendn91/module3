CONTINENTAL FUTSAL CHAMPIONSHIP 2022
As a part of the preparation for the 2022 AFC Futsal Championship in Kuwait, Thailand will host an international friendly tournament from September 11 to 16 The 6 participating teams (rank) include :

  • Iran (6)
  • Morocco (9)
  • Finland (18)
  • Thailand (20)
  • Vietnam (40)
  • Angola (46)

The teams are divided into 2 groups, each group have 3 teams.
The top 2 teams advance to the Semi-finals.
